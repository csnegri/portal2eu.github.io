Who are we?

A group of young PhD students and University researchers, with an interdisciplinary ppproach

We strive to improve digital public consultations in the European Union. Our project envisions a robust citizen engagement in democratic processes. This is why we have set up a multidisciplinary group, having computer scientists, communication experts, lawyers, and many more on board.

We have come up with a set of recommendations for the EU’s ‘Have Your Say!’ platform to make it even better. Our solution is simple: more accessibility, outreach, and transparency.

We live in the digital age and we need citizen engagement to meet 21st century challenges. Portal2EU is our story.
