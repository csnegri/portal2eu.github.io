The Portal2EU team is coming to Berlin

We are thrilled to announce that the Portal2EU group, alongside our fellow Europaeum scholars, will be presenting the result of our 18-month work at a large-scale conference in the vibrant city of Berlin, Germany, at the premises of Freie Universität Berlin from 25 to 27 September 2023.

In our presentation, our team members will delve into the topic of Improving EU Digital Public Consultations Processes. Specifically, we will talk about digital participation within the European Union and the challenges of the Have Your Say platform. Moreover, we will introduce our policy recommendations concerning 3 key pillars – Accessibility, Transparency, and Outreach. At the end of the presentation, we will take questions from the audience.

We are very excited to share our policy proposal with a wider audience of academics, policy makers and other professionals, and also to finally learn more about the work our colleagues have done over the course of the Europaeum Scholars Programme.

Nathalie Koubayova

PhD(c) Media and Communication Studies - Charles University’s
