Our Team

Where interdisciplinary finds its place

Carolina Gerli

PhD(c) Political and Social Sciences Alma Mater Studiorum – Universitá di Bologna ...

She investigates how public organizations use AI to tackle corruption in public procurement. She holds a MSc in Management of Public Organizations and a BSc in Business Administration from Bocconi University.

Claudia Negri-Ribalta

Doctor in Computer Science - Université Paris 1 Panthéon-Sorbonne ...

Expert in privacy and socio-technical security requirements engineering. She is a research associate at the University of Luxembourg and president of the Latin American NGO OptIA. She holds a Msc in Computer Science and a 5 year degree in Political Science.

Cyprian Liske

PhD(c) International Law - Jagiellonian University Doctoral School in the Social Sciences ...

His research focuses on the nexus between sustainable development and international trade law in the context of EU external policy. He holds an MA in Law (Jagiellonian University) and a BA in Business Linguistics (Tischner European University).

Florence Felsheim

PhD(c) in Classics - University of St Andrews ...

Her research interests include the political landscape of ancient Greece and Rome, gendered dynamics, and multiculturalism in the ancient world.

Hasan Akintug

PhD(c) in Political Science - Centre for Nordic Studies at the University of Helsinki ...

He studies the practice of paradiplomacy by self-governing island territories within the Nordic region.

Jan Farfal

PhD(c) History - University of Oxford ...

Also Research Assistant to Timothy Garton. Co-founder of the Club Alpbach Poland, visiting fellow at the Institute for Human Sciences (IWM) in Vienna.

Keith Prushankin

PhD(c) in Political Science - Freie Universität Berlin ...

His research expertise includes populism, post-communism, and great power politics.

Kseniia Soloveva

PhD(c) International Law - Grotius Centre for International Legal Studies, Leiden University ...

She graduated with a bachelor’s degree in law (with distinction) from the National Research University Higher School of Economics (Moscow, Russia) and an Advanced LL.M. in Public International Law (cum laude) from Leiden University.

Nathalie Koubayova

PhD(c) Media and Communication Studies - Charles University’s ...

In her research, Nathalie investigates the effects of different framing of the chatbot’s disclosure to the users in a text-based interface. Nathalie earned her Research Master’s degree in Communication Science at the University of Amsterdam.
